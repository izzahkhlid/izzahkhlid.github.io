Experience

- Improve my soft skills by doing part-time job! -


I’ve started to do part-time job when I was 18 while waiting for my SPM’s result. One of purposes I work on a part time basis is to get out of my comfort zone such as improve my communication skill, time management skill, and improve my finance management. At Coolblog, I worked as a waitress. My communication skills increased significantly because I was always approaching customers and conversing with them in a non-sequential manner. For example, prior to this, I was too afraid to approach strangers. I'm unable to make eye contact with everyone I've spoken with.


- My second part-time job -


During the last semester's break, I started my second part-time job. I keep choosing waitressing as a part-time career because I enjoy serving people and believe it is a very simple profession for me. In the city where I live, I work in one of the city's most well-known cafes. Nina Café is the name of the restaurant. Everyone will enjoy the cafe. This is due to the fact that this cafe is relatively quiet and ideal for a proper meal, lunch with colleagues, or breakfast with friends.



This is a pictures of my teammates. I enjoyed every single day I worked there since I worked in a pleasant setting with a supportive team and a caring management. Being around them taught me to be a better person who understands the importance of being kind to everyone, not just our clients.
