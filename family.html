Family

- My Big Family -


(2015)

Based on the picture above, it is clearly show that I have two sisters and two brothers and I'm the youngest in the family. Other than that, picture above shows that I've one sister in law and one small and cute of niece.

On 2011, my eldest brother getting married to her wife and now they have two pretty and cute daughters.

My house did not have any wedding ceremony for a couple of years. 8 years after the wedding feast of my elder brother, my second brother who is wearing a glasses in that picture told my mom that he want to get married on 2018.

One year after my second brother's wedding, my second sister who is standing beside me also announce to the family that she want to set up a wedding ceremony since she already have a job.

Despite the risk of a covid-19 pandemic, after the goverment announce that several place can set up a wedding ceremony, my eldest sister also get married.

I now have four nieces as a result of my brother and sister's marriage, with a fifth on the way.
