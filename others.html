OTHERS

- My Favourite Dessert -



Nestlé releases ice cream made out of REAL Musang King durian. The taste of this ice cream exactly same with the taste of durian Musang King. It is true that there are already durian ice creams out there in the market by other ice cream makers, but what makes Nestlé's a lot more unique is the fact that the durian is real. There's a catch: they're only available for RM25 each. In Malaysia, only hypermarkets and supermarkets sell Nestlé's Musang King durian ice cream.

To be honest, this Musang King ice cream is highly recommended especially to durian lovers out there. Have a try! You will not wasted your money on this 'king' thingy.
